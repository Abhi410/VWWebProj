CLASS ROOM

The advent of Computer has transformed the age-old industrial society into a modern Information Society. Now, this technology is creating Knowledge Technology (KT), which would transform Information Society into Knowledge Society (KS). In Knowledge Society, an extremely strong workforce of creative and innovative knowledge workers would be required to create the knowledge products. Accordingly, Computer Science Department at JEMTEC aims at to play a lead role in national vision of creating the futuristic knowledge economy through creativity and innovation in Information and Knowledge Technologies.

JEMTEC is the proud owner of a state of the art fully air conditioned computer department with more than 500 nodes equipped with latest software and hardware. Internet connectivity is provided through Fiber optic 2 Mbps LAN. The entire campus is connected with the Local Area Network.
