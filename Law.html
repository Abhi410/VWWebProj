Overview

JIMS Engineering Management Technical Campus School of Law, (JEMTEC) is affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi & Approved by the Bar Council of India, is a leading and top ranked law college in Delhi & NCR .The college was established to provide multi-disciplinary teaching and research opportunities to the students of BA-LLB & BBA-LLB.

We, at JEMTEC, focus more on professional Skill Development Activities of the students along with the classroom teaching to help students to get a lucrative career after their law studies.

College has globally recognized course to give exposure on theoretical and practical aspect of Law as students can learn about Constitutional Laws ?Civil and Criminal Procedure Code and much more.

Course Objective

  • The program aims to fulfill the following objectives:
  • To focus on practical knowledge for lucrative career after their legal studies.
  • To promote legal research practices amidst students.
  • To provide high quality socio-legal and management based education for holistic development in students.
  • To develop sensitive legal professionals towards the emerging socio-legal issues in the society.

Course Highlights

  • Highly qualified and experienced faculty.
  • Focus on holistic development of every student
  • Exposure to Intra, National, International Moot Court Competitions.
  • Skill based program focusing on communication, advocacy and analytical skills .
  • Focus on developing investigative thinking and problem solving approach among students .
  • Exposure to participate in competitions held at the national and international level .
  • Opportunity for students to participate in activities like jail visits and legal Aid camps .
  • Regular interaction with top lawyers and legal firms for practical exposure .
  • Electronic Database of Manupatra and Computer lab with Wi-Fi facility .
  • Internship in reputed law firms and companies .
  • Rich Library with law journals.
  • Real life experience sharing by Judges, advocates and other Eminent Personalities.

PROGRAM SPECIFIC OUTCOMES (PSOs)

PSO-1: To prepare the professionals for career as lawyers, in non litigation field, judiciary, law officers by exposing them to the concepts, application of legal education & research.
PSO-2: To lead a successful career in corporate, litigation, judiciary etc by developing awareness among them in various legal dimensions to solve legal problems.
PSO-3: To develop ethically responsible law professional who can impart justice to the society.
