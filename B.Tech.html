Engineering (B.Tech.)

B.Tech. is a 4-year full-time undergraduate program affiliated to Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (GGSIPU), Delhi and is approved by All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE).

Following is the Seats available in B.Tech. Program:

S.NO NAME OF THE BRANCH DURATION SEATS
1 Computer Science & Engineering 4 Years 180
2 Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AI & ML) 4 Years 60
3 Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI & DS) 4 Years 60

Overview

Bachelor of Technology is a four years programme, spread over eight-semesters. The programme comprises of various core and elective subjects interspersed with summer trainings and internships. All the semesters are a blend of intense classroom learning and hands learning in outstanding laboratories.

The first year of the programme is the foundation courses in core engineering subjects with blend of Humanities and Applied Sciences. Third Semester onwards, students undergo advanced studies and enhance their skills in their core branches.
